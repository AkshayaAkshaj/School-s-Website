iShare



The IShare program in K E Carmel jump started on Wednesday, June 8 th with the Inaugural Assembly for the new academic year.Students were encouraged to contribute what they could to help the poor and needy. The program has helped foster among students a sense of social responsibility, and proceeds have gone towards supporting education as well as medical treatment of the less fortunate members of society
